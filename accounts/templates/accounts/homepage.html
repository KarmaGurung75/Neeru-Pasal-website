Welcome To Neeru Passal

Neru Pasal is the local shop in Jomsom mustang, run by a mother-daughter team, which is one of many places to love visiting and doing local product shopping in mustang. It is known for selling local products like Apple jam, buckwheat flour, beans, dry fruits, etc. Those chocolates and candies on the counter attract more kids and sweet lovers.

Our Products

Buckwheat flour

Buckwheat flour, locally known as fapar ko pitho, is gluten-free packed with nutrition, amino acids, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. As a result, buckwheat consumption is linked to several health benefits, including improved blood sugar control and heart health. Buckwheat dido and pancakes are the famous food among the locals.

Chhurpi (yak cheese)

Chhurpi is a protein-rich cheese with a smoky flavor, soft and hard consistency, made from chhauri( female yak), which gradually becomes chewier the longer you gnaw at it. It's a favorite snack among the locals. There are two varieties of chhurpi, a soft variety usually consumed as a side dish with rice and butter tea, whereas a hard one chewed like betel.

Peach (Apricots)

Locally known as Khurpani, these yellow-orange fruits rich in vitamins and minerals may be small. Still, they are incredibly nutritious and have many health benefits, such as improved digestion and eye health.
